About BYT Parking App

How the App Works

The BYT Parking App allows users to manage vehicle parking efficiently. Users can enter vehicle details, generate tickets, and scan QR codes for quick check-ins and check-outs.

Main Features:

  • Vehicle Entry & Exit Management
  • QR Code Generation & Scanning
  • Daily & Weekly Reports
  • Print & Save Tickets
